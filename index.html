About the House

A small kitchen
with a long memory.

Halal on Fire began with a family recipe that traveled from Istanbul to a corner storefront on Pond Springs Road. Our owner grew up watching her mother layer phyllo for baklava at dawn and marinate lamb the night before a feast. That patience is what you taste in every plate today.

We're proudly women-owned and fully halal. Every wrap, every dip, every pistachio-dusted bite is made in-house — no shortcuts, no reheats. The pita leaves the oven when you order. The shawarma turns slow, all day long.
